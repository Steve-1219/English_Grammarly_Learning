#### 十一、改错题

| 序号 | 句子 | 改正 |
|:---:|---|---:|
| 1. | She go to school by bus every day. | She **goes** to school by bus every day. |
| 2. | The earth moved around the sun. | The earth **moves** around the sun.（客观真理用一般现在时）|
| 3. | I will tell him when he will come. | I will tell him when he **comes**.（主将从现）|
| 4. | He has died for 5 years. | He has **been dead** for 5 years.（非延续性动词→延续性表达）|
| 5. | He has gone to Shanghai once. | He has **been to** Shanghai once.（去过已回来用 been to）|
| 6. | I have bought a new car last week. | I **bought** a new car last week.（有过去时间状语用一般过去时）|
| 7. | I leave tonight. | I **am leaving** tonight.（按计划用进行时表将来）|
| 8. | By the end of last year, we finished the project. | By the end of last year, we **had finished** the project.（过去的过去用过去完成时）|
| 9. | She is knowing the answer. | She **knows** the answer.（know 是状态动词，不用进行时）|
| 10. | He told me they will arrange a party the next day. | He told me they **would arrange** a party the next day.（过去将来时）|
