| 1. | Not only his friends but also he like playing basketball. | Not only his friends but also he **likes** playing basketball.（就近一致）|

| 3. | I get up in 6 o'clock every day. | I get up **at** 6 o'clock every day. |
| 4. | He ran to the station, but he didn't reach it. | He ran **towards** the station, but he didn't reach it. |
| 5. | He walked through the street quickly. | He walked **across** the street quickly. |
| 6. | Beside Tom, all the students took part in the sports meeting. | **Except** Tom, all the students took part in the sports meeting. |
